The arsenal

Six teeth. One bite.

Every capability below is built on Robinhood's official Agentic Trading rails — equities, sandboxed, and disconnectable in one tap.

01
Genuinely Autonomous
Define a strategy in plain English or Codex. The agent analyzes, decides, and places live equity trades on Robinhood — no babysitting.
Genuinely Autonomous
Wolfie runs on Robinhood's official MCP servers inside a dedicated, ring-fenced agentic account. The agent only touches the capital you fund it with. Push notification on every fill. One-tap disconnect, always.
02
A Pack, Not a Bot
Spin up as many agents as you want. Each one hunts a different signal — momentum, mean-reversion, news, sentiment.
A Pack, Not a Bot
Diversify across strategies the way a fund diversifies across desks. Allocate capital per agent, set independent risk limits, and let them run in parallel. When one sleeps, another hunts.
03
It Shows Its Work
Wolfie doesn't just trade — it narrates. Watch the signal, the rationale, the risk math, and the order, in real time.
It Shows Its Work
No black box. Every decision streams its reasoning chain: what fired the signal, why it sized the position, where the stop sits. Train agents on datasets you choose and audit the logic line by line.
04
1,500+ Sources, Live
Wolfie ingests over 1,500 news feeds in real time and pipes the signal straight into every agent's decision loop.
1,500+ Sources, Live
Filings, financial wires, tech press, regulatory updates, social chatter — parsed, scored, and fed live. Your agents react to what's moving the tape before the headline finishes loading.
05
Your Data Stays Home
Wolfie stores nothing in the cloud but your billing. Trades move directly between the app and Robinhood.
Your Data Stays Home
Local-first by design. Your positions, strategies, and account data live on your machine — never our servers. The only thing we keep is what we need to bill you. Nothing personal ever transits our infrastructure.
06
Backtest Before Blood
Replay any strategy against historical data with realistic slippage and fees before a single real dollar moves.
Backtest Before Blood
Validate the hunt before you let it loose. Simulate against historical market data with realistic commissions, slippage, and fills. Tune the parameters, prove the edge, then deploy with conviction.
